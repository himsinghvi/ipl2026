Predictions: How To & FAQs
How To Use Predictions Page
  1. Review each upcoming match card and compare both team win percentages.
  2. Use the win-probability bar and favored team badge for quick interpretation.
  3. Open Match details for deeper team and player context.
  4. When logged in, pick your winner, set confidence (0-100), and click Save My Pick.
  5. If you already saved a pick, update it anytime before match start using Update My Pick.
FAQs

Q: Why can I not save picks?
A: You must be logged in from the Account panel and the match must be upcoming.

Q: What does confidence mean?
A: It is your own confidence score (0-100) for your selected winner. It does not change model output.

Q: Can I change my prediction later?
A: Yes. Saving again on the same match updates your existing pick.

Q: Why do probabilities not reach 100/0?
A: The model clamps outcomes to a realistic range, so extreme outputs are avoided.

Q: Are these betting odds?
A: No. These are heuristic analytics predictions generated from weighted team factors.
