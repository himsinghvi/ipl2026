Fantasy: How To & FAQs
How To Play Fantasy
  1. Login or signup from the Account panel.
  2. Open an upcoming match from Create Your Team.
  3. Select exactly 11 players, then choose one Captain and one Vice-Captain.
  4. Follow roster constraints (maximum 7 players from one real team).
  5. Submit your team and track points on leaderboard and profile pages after matches complete.
FAQs

Q: How many teams can I create per match?
A: One team per user per match. Re-submitting replaces your previous team for that match.

Q: Why is team submit failing?
A: Common reasons are not selecting exactly 11 players, missing captain/vice-captain, or exceeding 7 players from one side.

Q: How are points calculated?
A: Batting, bowling, and fielding actions earn points. Captain gets 2x and vice-captain gets 1.5x.

Q: When do points update?
A: Fantasy points are tied to completed matches and scorecard data, then reflected in leaderboard totals.

Q: Can I play without logging in?
A: You can browse the page, but creating or saving fantasy teams requires login.

Full Fantasy Points Logic (Implemented Rules)

Batting points
+1 per run, +1 per four, +2 per six.
Milestones: +4 (25+), +8 (50+), +16 (100+).
Duck: -2 if dismissed for 0 (no duck penalty when not out / did not bat).
Strike-rate adjustment (only when balls faced >= 10): +6 (SR >= 170), +4 (SR >= 150), -2 (SR < 80), -4 (SR < 60).

Bowling points
+25 per wicket, +12 per maiden over.
Wicket haul bonus: +8 (3 wickets), +12 (4 wickets), +16 (5+ wickets).
Economy adjustment (only when overs bowled >= 2): +6 (eco < 5), +4 (eco < 6), -2 (eco > 10), -4 (eco > 11).

Fielding points
+8 per catch, +12 per run-out.

Multiplier rule
Player total is calculated first. Then Captain gets 2x; Vice-Captain gets 1.5x (stored as integer after multiplication).

Scoring Examples

Example 1 (Captain): 52(30), 6 fours, 2 sixes, 1 catch.
Batting = 52 + 6 + 4 + 8 (50 bonus) + 6 (SR 173.3) = 76; Fielding = 8; Subtotal = 84; Captain 2x = 168.

Example 2 (Vice-Captain Bowler): 4 overs, 24 runs, 3 wickets, 1 maiden.
Bowling = 75 + 8 (3W bonus) + 12 (maiden) = 95; economy is 6.0 so no eco bonus/penalty; Vice-captain 1.5x = 142 (integer).
